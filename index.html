About

Located at 1450 Atlantic Ave in Brooklyn, LN shoes can be found within a Conoco gas station.

In an effort to give back to our community we do not sell anything online, nor do we have a phone number. However, feel free to stop by and check out our limited selection.

Open seven days of the week, our hours of operation are really whatever we feel like.
